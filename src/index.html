About XIRI [zeer-ee]

XIRI is an independent UI/UX and development company based in Denver, CO. Our approach bridges the disciplines of design and technology to craft interactive experiences that people love to use. With over 15 years of experience in UI and web, we’ve worked with just about every size and type of client from all industries. At XIRI, we build brands, develop sites and drink a lot of coffee in the process.

Our Services

What does XIRI offer in the way of services?

Website Development

We are focused on crafting pixel-perfect and standards compliant solutiuons. We are passionate about clean and elegant code and continually strive to explore new technologies and techniques in modern web development.

Mobile First Design

Now more than ever, most of us access our day-to-day content through a mobile device. We specialize in creating bold responsive layouts that help our clients take full advantage of the rapidly changing mobile devices market.

User Interface

We focus on creating intuitive and aesthetically pleasing User Interface designs that give our clients and customers simple and enjoyable ways to interact with a product or service. Every solution is and should be unique.

User Experience

We never forget that we are designing for people. We strive to help all of our clients understand their customers and key target demographics in order to make better decisions that promote success and growth for their online presence.

Focus on Performance

On average you only have a few precious seconds to engage and retain a visitor to your site. We focus on lean code that promotes performance and quick load times. Desktop or mobile, your users shouldn't be staring at that spinner.

Accessibility

Did you know that there are a wide range of users who simply can't use most sites due to accessbiility concerns? We bake accessibility and usability into every site, making your users happier and widening your customer base.

1

Analysis

The most important step in successful project completion is preparation. By communication with our clients extensively (phone, email, chat) and by asking the right questions we obtain all of the information that will be required for our team to accomplish the goals.

2

Design

We produce a design mock up based on the information that we obtain. We test several approaches and refine the prototype through several editing stages based on your feedback. Once the prototype is approved we design the rest of the pages and go through the same process.

3

Development

We convert static page designs into web ready HTML5 and CSS format. Afterwards we integrate the markup into a CMS and build required functionality. We go through another round of revisions prior to moving to the final stage. Throughout this stage of process our work can be observer on the development server.

4

Deploy

We test completed website until we make sure there are no unexpected errors and everything is working accordingly. Once it is tested we transfer the website to client’s hosting and set it up. We provide free 60 days of support, that includes error fixes and edits, after the transfer.
